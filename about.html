Our Story

Discover the journey of Beauty & Bloom, from a small Parisian boutique to a global beauty destination celebrating natural beauty and Parisian elegance.

Our Humble Beginnings

From Paris with Love

Beauty & Bloom was founded in 2010 by childhood friends Amélie and Sophie, who shared a passion for beauty and a vision to create cosmetics that celebrated natural beauty rather than masking it.

Starting with a small boutique on a charming side street in Paris, they curated a collection of high-quality, natural-ingredient cosmetics that quickly gained popularity among Parisian women seeking elegance with simplicity.

What began as a local treasure soon attracted attention from beauty enthusiasts worldwide, drawn to the brand's Parisian elegance, commitment to quality, and philosophy that beauty should bloom naturally.

Today, with our flagship store on the iconic Champs-Élysées, we continue to honor our founders' vision while expanding our reach to bring Parisian beauty to the world.

Our Philosophy

At Beauty & Bloom, we believe that true beauty comes from confidence and self-care. Our mission is to provide products that enhance your natural features while caring for your skin.

We reject the notion that beauty requires complexity. Instead, we embrace simplicity, quality, and the power of natural ingredients to create products that perform beautifully while respecting your skin.

Every product in our collection is developed with our three core principles in mind: purity, performance, and pleasure. We carefully select each ingredient, test every formula, and design each package with intention.

Our commitment extends beyond beauty products—we're dedicated to sustainable practices, ethical sourcing, and giving back to our community through various initiatives.

Our Core Values

Natural Ingredients

We prioritize natural, skin-friendly ingredients that are both effective and gentle. Our formulas are free from harsh chemicals and always cruelty-free.

Quality First

Every product undergoes rigorous testing to ensure the highest quality standards. We never compromise on performance or safety.

Empowering Beauty

We believe beauty should empower, not pressure. Our products are designed to enhance confidence and celebrate individuality.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint through sustainable packaging, ethical sourcing, and responsible manufacturing.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At Beauty & Bloom, we believe beauty should never come at the expense of our planet. That's why we've implemented comprehensive sustainability initiatives across every aspect of our business.

From responsibly sourced ingredients to eco-friendly packaging, we're constantly working to reduce our environmental impact while maintaining the quality and luxury our customers expect.
